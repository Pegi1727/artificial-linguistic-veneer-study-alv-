
# Appendix A: Artificial Linguistic Veneer (ALV) Rubric
‌
This rubric is designed to assess the extent to which participants’ AI-assisted written texts demonstrate surface-level linguistic sophistication without corresponding conceptual, argumentative, or disciplinary depth.
‌
| Dimension | Description | Score (1-5) |
|:---|:---|:---:|
| **Lexical Sophistication** | Use of advanced vocabulary and polished wording without equivalent analytical substance. | [ ] |
| **Syntactic Fluency** | Grammatically smooth and structurally refined language that may exceed the writer’s oral control. | [ ] |
| **Generic Academic Framing** | Reliance on formulaic academic phrases and broad claims without context-specific argumentation. | [ ] |
| **Argumentative Thinness** | Lack of strong reasoning or evidence-based explanation despite fluent appearance. | [ ] |
| **Voice Displacement** | Detachment from the writer’s authentic disciplinary voice or personal stance. | [ ] |
‌
### Scoring Interpretation
- **5–9 (Very Low ALV):** Strong writer ownership and alignment between form and substance.
- **18–25 (High/Very High ALV):** Fluent expression masking significant weaknesses in reasoning or ownership.

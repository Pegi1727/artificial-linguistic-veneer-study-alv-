# Appendix C: Oral Defense Performance Protocol (ODP)
‌
This protocol is used to evaluate conceptual ownership and critical understanding through a semi-structured oral interview.
‌### Assessment Dimensions (Scored 1-5)

Conceptual Understanding: Ability to explain key ideas in their own words.
Argument Justification: Ability to defend claims with relevant support.
Textual Ownership: Familiarity with the specific decisions made in the text.
Response Coherence: Logical flow and clarity of oral responses.
Critical Engagement: Ability to respond to spontaneous follow-up questions.
‌
### Sample Interview Questions
Can you briefly explain the main argument of your written text?
Why did you choose this particular focus or position?
What are the key concepts in your text, and how are they related?
Can you explain one of the main claims in your writing in your own words?
What evidence or reasoning supports your argument?
How did you decide whether an AI-generated suggestion was appropriate?
Is there any part of your text that you would explain differently now?
What limitations can you identify in your argument?
